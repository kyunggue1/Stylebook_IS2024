This is for the mean opinion score listening test.

It consits of 30 samples, each sample has a source file and 4 system outputs.
If you listen too much artifact, please turn of step.ext and start again with proper setting.
You can continue from where you stopped. 
Please see the setting in ppt and contact me if it is not working. 

Please evaluate the overall speech quality in 5 scale score.
Instructions for the 5 scale is as follows. 

Score		How natural			How robotic
5: Excellent,	Completely natural speech, 	Imperceptible
4: Good,	Mostly natural speech, 		Perceptible but not annoying
3: Fair,	Equally natural and unnatural,	Perceptible and slightly annoying
2: Poor,	Monstly unnatural,		Annoying, but not objectionable
1: Bad,		Completely unnatural speech,	Very annoying and objectionable

When you start the experiment, check on below conditions.

Randomize Conditions
Play Mono As Stereo
Approve Scores

Thank you for participation. 

### Tips ###
Focus on the quality only, don't need to compare relative quality.
Give 100 to reference file. 
If none of three samples have 100 score, it cannot go to next sample.
